Ukrainian Vintage Boutique

Step into our exquisite vintage boutique at the Annual Ukrainian Summer Picnic! Explore a curated selection of beautiful, authentic, and refined items that are sure to captivate you. From stunning jewelry to elegant home decor, our boutique offers unique treasures that blend timeless charm with Ukrainian heritage.

Ukrainian Food

Get ready to dive into a delicious spread of authentic Ukrainian dishes and refreshing drinks! From savory classics to sweet treats, our menu has it all, prepared with love and authenticity. Whether you’re craving hearty meals or have a sweet tooth, there’s something for everyone!

Petrykivka Art

Immerse yourself in the vibrant art of Petrykivka painting at our captivating workshop together with Sofia Ogorodnyk, a renowned expert in the craft! Join us to create stunning Petrykivka artworks, embracing Ukraine's rich cultural heritage and intricate folk art traditions.

Ukrainian Block Printing Workshop

Explore the art of 'vybiyka' at this amazing workshop! Together with Mariya Vladymyrova,a celebrated master of the art, learn the traditional techniques of printing patterns using dyes applied to hand-carved wooden blocks and stamping them on fabric with oil art paints. Join us to create beautiful ethnic designs!
